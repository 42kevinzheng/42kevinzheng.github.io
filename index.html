About Me (Gap In Resume*)

Augest 2020 - Present

I am 1 of 5 partners in a small restaurant business. 3 partners are family including myself and the other 2 are friends of the family. So, this is not exactly a family business. In Feb 2020, We had opened a Japanese All You Can Eat (AYCE) restaurant http://ichiban8889.com/ located in Conway, SC.

In March 2020, the world was hit by news of a catastrophic contagious disease known as COVID-19. In the following weeks, the governor of South Carolina told all dine-in restaurants to close down until further notice. During this time period, a large number of our employee was laid off, either, they quit out of fear of catching the virus or we had to let them go because the restaurant was closed down. Two months later we've reopened.

Because, we were short-staff and unable to find new workers, I have decided to work full-time working more than 80 hours to do anything that the restaurant needed me to do, such as, manager, server, cook, sushi chef, and other roles. I have originally intended  to work a few months and then begin looking for a software engineering/developer job. However, I have underestimated how fast time can pass by and before I knew it, it was already 2021.

As of March 2021, business is improving, thanks to a combination of vaccines, government relief programs, and people being tired of staying indoors all day. We were able to find new workers and I can devote more time to learning new technologies, side-projects, and beginning my software career.
